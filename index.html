2400 acres on a single plot

We have a mixture of hardwood bottoms, old and new pines, several creeks, and over 1.5 miles of powerline running through the center of the property.

Green fields

Established food plots throughout the property in addition to strategically placed plots with shooting houses across the powerline. We also do some Spring planting to keep the Deer happy

All Game or just Turkey

With a full membership you can hunt all game (Deer, Turkey, Rabbit, Squirrel, and Coyote) for $1450. If you want to just hunt Turkey you can get a partial membership for $725.

Check out some of our Game Cam pics

Camp House

It ain't easy on the eyes, but it sleeps good. The camp house has power, water, full kitchen, 2 full bathrooms, and plenty of room for you to bunk down. We also have several spots for campers with power available

When you're not in the woods

We have a firebarrel and cookout shed where members sit around the barrel watching Football and spinning tales of the big one that got away.
