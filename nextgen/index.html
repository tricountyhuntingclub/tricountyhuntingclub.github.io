2400 Acres

Near Highland Home, Alabama, Our club has a mixture of hardwood bottoms, old and new pines, several creeks, and over 1.5 miles of powerline running through the center of the property.

What do you like to hunt ?

Our annual dues are $1450. Membership gives you access to hunt all game: Deer, Turkey, Rabbit, Squirrel, and Coyote. Check out some of our game camera pictures

Room to hunt

Food plots throughout the property in addition to shooting houses across the powerline. There's also plenty of space to hang your stand in the Pines and Hardwoods.

Camp house

The camp house is on 2 acres with power, water, full kitchen, 2 full bathrooms, and plenty of room for you to bunk down. We also have a couple spots for campers with power available

Fire Barrel

A hunting club has to have a fire barrel! We have one and cookout shed where members sit around the barrel watching Football and spinning tales of the big one that got away.
